# 👟 Adidas Sales Analysis Dashboard

![image alt](https://github.com/MAUZZAM123/Adidas-Sales-Analysis-Performance-Dashboard/blob/7795be0ec592d8b4ca7ab4c12811a90b8d2a1120/Screenshot%202025-12-03%20103959.png)

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive **Adidas Sales Analysis Dashboard** built in Power BI to analyze sales performance, profitability, retailer contribution, and geographic sales distribution.

The dashboard transforms raw sales data into meaningful business insights through interactive visualizations and KPI tracking, enabling stakeholders to make informed, data-driven decisions. The project focuses on sales analysis, trend monitoring, and business performance evaluation using Power BI.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The primary objective of this project is to analyze Adidas sales data and identify key business trends related to revenue, profit, product performance, retailers, and sales channels.

The dashboard helps answer important business questions such as:

* Which sales channel generates the highest revenue?
* Which product categories contribute the most profit?
* Which retailers drive the highest sales?
* How are sales distributed across different cities?
* What are the sales and profit trends over time?

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains Adidas sales transaction data, including information related to:

| Column Category     | Description                          |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Product Information | Product categories and sales details |
| Retailers           | Retailer-wise sales performance      |
| Sales Methods       | Online, Outlet, and In-store sales   |
| Geographic Data     | City and regional sales distribution |
| Revenue Metrics     | Sales amount and profit              |
| Units Sold          | Quantity of products sold            |
| Time Data           | Date-based sales analysis            |

---

## 🔧 Data Cleaning & Transformation

Data preparation was performed using **Power Query Editor** within Power BI.

The following transformations were applied:

* Removed duplicate records
* Checked and handled missing values
* Corrected data types
* Standardized column formats
* Cleaned inconsistent data entries
* Prepared data model for reporting and analysis

## 📈 Dashboard Highlights

### 📦 Sales Method-wise Units Sold & Margin

Analyze product sales volume and profitability across different sales channels.

### 🥧 Total Sales by Sales Method

Compare sales performance between:

* Online
* Outlet
* In-store

### 📈 Profit & Sales Trends

Track revenue and profit performance across different calendar periods to identify seasonal patterns and growth opportunities.

### 🏬 Retailer-wise Sales Performance

Evaluate retailer contribution and identify top-performing sales partners.

### 🌍 Geographic Sales Distribution

Visualize city-wise sales performance through map-based reporting.

Compare profitability across different Adidas product categories.

### 📊 KPI Summary Panel

Provides a quick overview of key business metrics and performance indicators.

## 💡 Key Insights

* Online sales contribute the largest share of total revenue.
* Men's Apparel and Women's Apparel generate the highest operating profit.
* Sales and profit trends indicate peak business performance during mid-month periods.
* Sales are distributed across 52 cities, demonstrating strong market presence.
* A small group of retailers contributes a significant percentage of overall sales.
* Product category analysis helps identify the most profitable product segments.
